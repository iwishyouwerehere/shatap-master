We know how important it is to stay protected, feel like being at home, with your sweet peluche and your hot chocolate burning up yourself. That's why we developed a chat system where you only care about sending what you want to send, no need to worry.

security
Security

Client-side encryption
where you decide
your security.

But how can we achieve this? By joining a chat you choose a key which will be used to securely encrypt your messages. No one can understand or force them unless they have the same key as you. Neither us can access this data.

access_time
Time

You can hack us,
if you have it enough.

That's why we decided to start a countdown time every time a chat is created, so that you leave no tracks. Also, if you're inactive, you get asked to rejoin the chat. Every time someone join a chat it gets a brand new randomly generated, beautiful, username. No one can track you or know if it was actually you.
